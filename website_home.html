Website Builds

Building websites is relatively easy online, we have access to platforms like WIX that allows us to build websites in a few clicks - The problem is mallaebility.
Businesses and individuals all know the ease of making and maintaining a website with modern day online applications. But, you still want and need the website to function in an exact manner for your business and to represent your business. That's done through tailoring your website.

Coffee Shop

This website was created during my time at Code Nation as part of a design project. While not my best work, I chose to showcase it as it highlights how far my design skills have come since then. It also wasn’t my first web project, as I had previously developed complete systems during my university studies.

The objective was to create a business concept and design a static website to represent it. The site needed to present the company to an online audience and include a marketing plan. We were given roughly a week to complete the project, so efficiency and clear design choices were key.

The final product turned out quite elegant and demonstrated my growing confidence in layout design. Through this build, I learned to use the CSS ‘Grid’ property, which enables repeating sections within a layout and ensures a consistent, well-aligned appearance across the website.

Takeaway Website

This project was created in my spare time as part of exploring how technology can solve real business challenges. I often look at local businesses to identify areas for digital improvement, which helps me stay sharp at spotting opportunities and understanding technology’s impact on operations.

The goal was to design a modern version of a takeaway website inspired by platforms like Uber Eats and Just Eat. I wanted to enhance the overall look and feel while improving user experience and convenience—key factors for any business operating in today’s fast-paced, service-driven world.

The outcome was a functional website where users can log in, place orders, print receipts, and save their details, combining practical features with a clean, user-focused design.
